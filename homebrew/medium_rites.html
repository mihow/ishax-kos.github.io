Medium Rites

  • Multiclassing:

    Ghost Touch & Ki strikes

    Having both the Ghost touch and Ki strikes abilities allows you to treat any monk weapon you hold or throw as magical for purposes of overcoming resistance and immunity to physical damage.

    Ghost Touch

    A creature with the "Incorporeal Movement" Ability cannot use this ability to better move through your square. your unarmed strikes count as magical against creatures that are listed as being resistant to "Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing From Nonmagical Attacks" (ghosts and some elementals for example). You can grapple creatures that are immune to the grappled condition.

  • Expanded Repertoire

    Choose a type of creatures from celestials, elementals, or fiends. Add this type of creature to your repitoire. This choice is permanent, but you may take this rite multiple times. You also gain an additional language depending on your choice according to the table "language gained".
    Language Gained
    Type Language
    Celestials Celestial
    Elemental Primordial
    Fiend Abyssal or Infernal

  • Two as One

    You dedicate yourself to a single spirit. When channeling this spirit, you gain your full proficiency bonuses when using the spirit's skills as well as twice your proficiency bonus in skills and with weapons you both have proficiency with. With all other spirits you must take on a taboo, or the normal benifits of channeling are not gained. Time spent channeling your primary spirit does not count towards your maximum time, but running out of time while channeling another spirit will prevent you from channeling your primary spirit.

  • Symbiosis

    When you take on a taboo for a channeled spirit, your body physically changes, taking on prominant feature of its appearance (you need not take on its appearance in full). In addition, you can cast the disguise self spell at will, but only to appear as your channeled spirit. The image appears at full scale despite the limitations of the spell. When you choose this rite you must choose two options from attack, defence, movement, or senses this decision cannot be changed. You gain one of the following benifits depending on your choice:

    Attack: If the spirit had any natural weapons, you gain those weapons. These deal 1d6 damage, and have the finesse property.

    Defence: If the spirit had any natural armor, you gain natural armor and have an armor class of 13 + plus your Dexterity modifier.

    Movement: If the spirit had a climb or swim speed, you temporarily gain a coresponding 30ft climb or swim speed, as well as any abilities that improve its ability to move on specific surfaces such as webs or difficult terrain.

    Sense: If the spirit has an ability that gives it advantage on wisdom (perception) checks in certain scenarios such as keen scent, you gain that ability.

    You can take rite multiple times, but you must choose two new options each time.

  • Greater Symbiosis

    Prerequisite: 7th level, Symbiosis feature

    You gain a number of abilities depending on what choices you made for the Symbiosis rite.

    Attack: The given natural weapons instead deal 2d6 damage.

    Defence: The given natural armor give you and armor class of 15 + your Dexterity modifier.

    Movement: If the spirit had a fly or swim speed, you gain a coresponding 60ft fly or swim speed. Your land speed also increases by 10 feet.

    Sense: If the spirit had a special sense such as darkvision or tremorsense, you gain that sense with a range of 60 feet. You can speak and directly understand your spirit's languages.

  • Mortal Blade

    Prerequisite: 9th level

    You can harden your hands and use them as natural weapons a number of times equal to your wisdom modifier. They deal 2d6 damage and have the finesse property. If you have natural weapons already, your hands instead deal 2d8 damage. You regain all expended uses after a long rest.

    At 13th level, your hands deal 2d8 damage or 2d12 damage if you have natural weapons already.

  • Beast Speech

    You can cast Speak with Animals at will, without expending a spell slot.

    (See warlock "Beast Speech")

  • Collaboration

    Prerequisite: 5th level

    You have advantage on Constitution saving throws that you make to maintain your concentration on a spell.

    (See warlock "Eldritch Mind")

  • Voice of the Heart

    Prerequisite: Third Eye feature

    When you open your third eye, you can understand any language spoken to you. You can also cast detect thoughts once per use of the third eye feature.

  • Seer Sight

    Prerequisite: Third Eye feature

    When you open your third eye, you can see any creature within 60 feet of you that is within your line of sight regardless of invisibility, blindness, or lighting. You also have the ghost's ability to see 60 feet into an ethereal plane from it's host plane, or into a plane from it's ethereal plane.

  • Aura Sight

    You can cast Detect Magic at will, without expending a spell slot or material components.
